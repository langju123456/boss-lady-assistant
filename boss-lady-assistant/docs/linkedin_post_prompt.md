# LinkedIn Post Generator Prompt

Use the lessons in docs/06_lessons_learned.md to generate a LinkedIn post.

Writing style:

* Clear
* Reflective
* Founder-style
* Product-management focused
* Not too promotional
* Not too technical
* Focus on lessons, product thinking, customer discovery, and AI product development

Structure:

1. Start with a strong insight
2. Explain the original assumption
3. Explain what changed
4. Explain the product decision
5. End with a broader lesson about AI products, product management, or customer discovery

Avoid:

* Hype
* Buzzwords
* Overclaiming
* Fake metrics
* Saying the product is successful before validation

Example opening:

I started this project thinking I was building an AI tool.

After talking to real users, I realized I was wrong.

The real product is not AI.

The real product is the business outcome AI helps create.
