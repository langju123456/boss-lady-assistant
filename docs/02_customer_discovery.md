## Example Entry

### Interview Date

2026-06-13

### Interviewee

Founder's mother

### Business Type

Small beauty salon

### Business Size

Small owner-operated salon with a limited team.

### Main Pain Points

* Daily business data is not recorded in a structured way.
* Activity planning takes time and depends on personal experience.
* Marketing content for WeChat is repetitive and hard to prepare quickly.
* Business review is based on memory instead of clear numbers.

### Current Workflow

The salon mainly uses WeChat and personal experience. Customer communication, follow-up, activity reminders, and daily business judgment happen through informal conversations and memory rather than a structured digital system.

### Interesting Quotes

To be filled after real customer conversations.

### Potential Opportunities

* Natural language daily business recording
* Simple daily, weekly, and monthly business review
* Ready-to-use WeChat activity content
* Customer follow-up message generation
* Plain-language business advice

### Follow-up Questions

* What does the owner currently record every day?
* Which numbers does she actually care about?
* What activity formats have worked before?
* What customer follow-up messages feel natural in her voice?
* Would voice input be easier than typing?
